# Privacy Policy for FluentPing

**Last Updated:** [3/19/2026]

## 1. Introduction
FluentPing ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our desktop application.

## 2. Information We Collect
We collect the following types of information:

*   **License Information:** When you validate your license, we collect your license key.
*   **AI Interactions:** When you use AI features (e.g., generating flashcards, asking questions), the text of your queries is transmitted to our servers.
*   **Technical Data:** We automatically collect your IP address when you connect to our services (Licensing, AI, Updates).
*   **Search Data:** When you use the "Web Search" feature, your search query is sent to Google.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
*   **License Validation:** To verify your purchase and unlock features.
*   **AI Features:** To generate educational content and answer your questions.
*   **Updates:** To check for and deliver software updates.
*   **Improvement:** To monitor the stability and performance of our services.

## 4. Third-Party Service Providers (Data Processors)
We share data with the following third-party providers to operate the app:

*   **Vercel (USA):** Hosts our licensing and AI proxy servers. Your IP address and request data (license key, AI prompts) are processed by Vercel.
*   **GitHub (USA):** Used to check for software updates. Your IP address is visible to GitHub during these checks.
*   **Google (USA):** The "Web Search" feature uses Google Custom Search Engine. Google may collect your search queries, IP address, and set cookies on your device in accordance with [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 5. International Data Transfers
Your information is transferred to and processed in the United States, where our service providers (Vercel, GitHub, Google) are located. By using the app, you consent to this transfer.

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)
Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
*   Access the personal data we hold about you.
*   Request deletion of your data.
*   Opt-out of the sale of your personal data (we do not sell your data).

To exercise these rights, please contact us at: PINGPINGFLASHCARDS@PROTON.ME

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app signifies your acceptance of the changes.
